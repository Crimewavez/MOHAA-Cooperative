# MOHAA Cooperative

[![Discord](https://img.shields.io/discord/1344795200907403335?logo=discord&label=Discord&color=5865F2)](https://discord.gg/SA2scfnXUJ)

A cooperative gameplay mod for Medal of Honor: Allied Assault and OpenMOHAA.

Play through the singleplayer campaigns of Allied Assault, Spearhead, and Breakthrough together with friends.

Discord server is used only for community purposes and gathering playtesters / players to play in group. It is NOT for: development talk, coop bug reports. Use discussions and Issues.

## Planned Features

- Full cooperative playthrough of all three campaigns (Allied Assault, Spearhead, Breakthrough)
- Player synchronization across scripted singleplayer missions
- Compatible with [OpenMoHAA](https://github.com/openmoh/openmohaa) and the GOG War Chest edition

## Requirements

- Medal of Honor: Allied Assault War Chest - [GOG edition](https://www.gog.com/game/medal_of_honor_allied_assault_war_chest) (supported out of the box) or retail
- Optionally, [OpenMoHAA](https://github.com/openmoh/openmohaa) (open-source source port)
